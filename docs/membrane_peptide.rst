==========================
 Membrane peptide dataset
==========================

.. module:: MDAnalysisData.membrane_peptide

The :mod:`MDAnalysisData.membrane_peptide` module contains a 1-ns
equilibrium MD trajectory of a short synthetic Î±-helical
peptide in a DMPC membrane with explicit water solvent. It can be used
to study protein-lipid interactions and demonstrates the effect of
periodic boundary conditions on the atomic coordinates.
